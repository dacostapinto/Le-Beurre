Le Beurre

Bakery & Coffee House

French treats and culture meets the San Francisco city streets

Le Beurre, which translates to “The Butter” in French, pushes the boundaries of an American morning coffee run. Instead of a rushing workaholic lifestyle that most Americans are drowned in, Le Beurre’s ambiance encourages customers to sit, relax, and talk with friends while enjoying their croissants and espressos.
